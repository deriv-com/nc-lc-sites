How do I reset password for my Deriv Affiliate account?

To reset your password:

  1. Log in to your Affiliate dashboard.
  2. Go to "Your Account".
  3. Under “Edit account details”, enter your current password.
  4. Then, enter your new password and confirm password again.
  5. Click Update at the bottom of the page.

‍

If you have multiple accounts linked to your email, contact us via live chat to confirm which account you want to reset your password.

Account management
How can I set up or change the payment method in my Affiliate dashboard?

To update your payment method:

  1. Log in to your Affiliate dashboard.
  2. Navigate to the “Finances” section.
  3. Click on “Payment instructions”.
  4. Select your preferred payment method.
  5. Confirm your new payment method by saving the changes.
Account management
Where can I see my earnings?

To view your commissions:

  1. Log in to your Affiliate dashboard.
  2. Click on “Reports”.
  3. Select “Detailed activity report”.
  4. Set filters if required (e.g., time period).
  5. View or download your report.

What information do Affiliate reports provide?

Affiliate reports provide detailed insights such as:

  1. Hits and impressions
  2. Click-through rates
  3. Client trading activity
  4. Your commissions
  5. Client sign-up dates

How do I get my Deriv affiliate referral link?
  1. Log in to your affiliate dashboard.
  2. Click "Marketing" tab.
  3. Scroll down, select ‘Direct links’ on the left-hand side of your screen.
  4. Click on the link titled ‘Direct signup- EN’.
  5. Copy the link under ‘Landing page URL’ and share it with your clients
  6. You also may provide other referral links based on your clients preferred languages.

‍

For additional guidance, watch this video: How to get your referral link

Account management
How do I check which clients are linked to me?

To check your linked clients:

  1. Log in to affiliate dashboard.
  2. Go to "Reports" tab.
  3. Choose "Customer registrations"on left side on the bar.
  4. Filter the report period according to your clients' joined date.
  5. Click "display report".

How can I update my affiliate referral links?

You cannot update your affiliate referral links. We don't change affiliate links as this can affect clients.

Account management
Can I get the contact details of clients linked to my partner account?

No, client contact details are not accessible. In the Customer Registrations report, you can view only the client’s CR Account, join date, country, and subscribed plan.

Account management
How do I change my country of residence?

To change your residence, please provide a proof of address from your new residence country. It should show your name, address, and be dated within the last 12 months. Contact us via live chat to request the change.
