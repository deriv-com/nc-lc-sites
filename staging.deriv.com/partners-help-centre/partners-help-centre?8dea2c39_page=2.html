Who is eligible to become a Deriv Affiliate?

To be eligible as a Deriv Affiliate, you must:

  • Be 18 years old or older
  • Not reside in a FATF-restricted country

Anyone capable of referring traders is welcome to apply.

If you are any of the following, youll have an even better chance of referring potential clients:

  • Trading experts
  • Content creators (bloggers, YouTubers, social media influencers)
  • Software developers

What type of revenue models or commission plans do you offer for Affiliates?

We have 3 revenue models for Affiliates promoting Options:

  1. Revenue Share: Earn commissions up to 45% based on the monthly net revenue generated by your clients. The higher their net revenue, the higher your commission payout.
  2. Turnover: Earn up to 1.5% commission for each contract your clients purchase.
  3. Cost per acquisition (For EU clients only): Earn a USD 100 commission for each successful accumulative USD 100 deposit. Your referred client must open a real account using your tracking link and deposit USD 100 or more (one-time or accumulative).

For Affiliates promoting CFDs, refer to this question on CFD calculations.

How can I withdraw my Deriv IB commissions?

Your MT5 commissions will be credited to your MT5 Standard account daily, while commissions from Deriv X and Deriv cTrader are credited into your real Deriv account daily.

To withdraw your MT5 IB commissions, you'll need to transfer it to your Deriv account and make a withdrawal.

You can withdraw your IB commissions as soon as they are credited into your account. There is no required waiting period for withdrawals.

Quick help

How do I become a Deriv affiliate?

You'll need to complete an Affiliate application form on our Partners page.

  1. Go to our Partners page and click on "Join Now".
  2. Complete the Affiliate application form.
  3. Wait for approval, which typically takes 1-3 working days.

The approval process may take lesser time if you have a real Deriv account with the same email as your partner account application. If you do not have an account, you can sign up for one here.

How do I become an introducing broker (IB) with Deriv?

To become an IB, you need to:

  • Be an existing Deriv affiliate
  • Open a Deriv FIAT account
  • Have a real Deriv MT5 Standard account

Once you meet the IB programme requirements, contact us via live chat to apply.

For more details, watch our video guide.

How do I get my Deriv affiliate referral link?

  1. Log in to your affiliate dashboard.
  2. Click "Marketing" tab.
  3. Scroll down, select Direct links on the left-hand side of your screen.
  4. Click on the link titled Direct signup- EN.
  5. Copy the link under Landing page URL and share it with your clients
  6. You also may provide other referral links based on your clients preferred languages.

For additional guidance, watch this video: How to get your referral link

How are CFD commissions calculated?

CFD commissions calculations are based on the following, depending on which CFD platform your client trades on:

  • Turnover or trading lot size
  • The volume of your clients trades
