How to compete

Your task is to tackle one of our challenging problems by building a live, working prototype for one of the following fields, using AI, no-code or low-code tools. The exact challenges will be revealed during pre-hackathon briefing on 5 November.

Product enhancement

Harness AI solutions to develop smarter, more intuitive user experiences that adapt to market conditions and user needs.

Customer sentiment analysis

Use AI solutions to analyse client interactions, delivering actionable insights to improve satisfaction and prevent potential issues.

Real-time risk monitoring

Create AI-driven solutions for dynamic monitoring of market conditions, enabling more effective risk management.

Security and fraud detection

Apply AI solutions to identify security threats or fraudulent activities, ensuring a safer environment for users.

Operational efficiency

Explore AI solutions to improve efficiency, such as verifying document authenticity or automating processes to support fair practices.

FAQs

1. How do I apply to participate?

Simply click the "Join the challenge" button, complete the application form, and introduce your team. Only shortlisted teams will be invited to join the competition. We can’t wait to see what you’ll bring to the table.

2. Do I need to have a team to participate?

Yes, a team is required to join this hackathon. Your team should consist of 3 to 4 members, including at least one coder with AI expertise with AI/low-code development expertise.

3. I’m not a coder. Can I still join?

Absolutely! While your team must have a coder with AI/low-code development expertise, other skills (design, project management, research, etc.) are just as crucial.

4. Is the event open to non-Malaysians?

Yes, as long as you are currently residing in Malaysia with a valid PR, work, or student visa.

5. Is the event open to Deriv employees?

Yes, Deriv employees are welcome to join the challenge. We have a special category for Deriv employee teams.

6. Do participants need to come to Cyberjaya on the hackathon day? 

Yes, if they are shortlisted to compete, all participants must attend the hackathon in person at the venue (Deriv HQ, Cyberjaya) on 9–10 November 2024.

7. What if I need to rest during the hackathon?

No worries! You’re welcome to stay overnight at Deriv HQ and we will provide a safe space to rest. You can take power naps in between coding. Please bring your own sleeping bags, pillows, toiletries, etc.

8. Is food provided during the event?

Yes. We’ll offer full meals, a variety of snacks, and beverages throughout the event to keep you energised and focused as you work on your projects.

9. What do we need to hack?  

You’ll be developing an AI application for one of the five challenges listed on this webpage. A working prototype is required for the final demo.

10. Is there a registration fee?

No, it’s free to participate. All you need to do is apply to join, wait for the shortlist confirmation and bring your enthusiasm… and laptops!

11. How long is the hackathon?  

The event begins at 9:00 am on 9 November and will conclude by 5:00 pm on 10 November with the final demos and award ceremony.

12. What are the judging criteria?  

Judges will look for innovation, practicality, functionality, and design. Your team’s technical ability and presentation will also play a role in the evaluation.

13. How do we present our work?

Your team will demo the prototype live in front of judges at the venue. Submission details will be made clear during the event.

14. Where can I get the latest event updates?

Follow Deriv's LinkedIn page for the latest announcements and updates. You can also contact us at [email protected].

15. What should I bring to the hackathon?

Please bring your laptops, chargers, and any other tools you need.
