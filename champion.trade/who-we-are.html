Who we are

Champion Trader is an online trading service provider owned and operated by the Deriv Group, built upon 25 years of technological excellence and sharp customer focus. We serve as a one-stop hub where traders can access a variety of CFD trading platforms through a single, convenient account.

Our mission to make trading accessible to everyone drives our mobile-first strategy. Our platforms are optimized for mobile use and are designed to work efficiently on lower-end devices and with low internet connectivity, ensuring traders can access the markets anytime, anywhere, regardless of their technology or network limitations

Our values are the fabric of our culture

Integrity

We serve our customers with fairness and transparency. We settle all contracts by the book and speak plainly and truthfully.

Customer focus

We put the customer first and strive to build products that deliver the best customer experience. 

Competence

We value colleagues with the ability to use good judgement and an aptitude to learn and grow. 

Teamwork

We value team players that collaborate freely across departments with humility and ambition. 

Our principles are the framework for our decisions

Be reliable

We settle all contracts fairly, process all deposits and withdrawals promptly, and provide reliable support and a high-quality trading experience to all our customers.

Be fair

We treat all customers equitably, handle complaints with integrity, and offer competitive prices with no hidden costs and no artificial barriers on customer withdrawals.

Be transparent

We speak plainly to avoid ambiguity, disclose the terms of all contracts, and are clear about the risks of trading and how we make money. 

Be responsible

We don’t practise hard-selling, offer financial or trading advice, nor make promises of guaranteed returns. We don’t encourage vulnerable people to trade and implement controls to prevent any unlawful work.
